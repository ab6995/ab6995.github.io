RAG
Hybrid RAG Agent Assist

Agentic AI · Pinecone · Enterprise support

Led development of an enterprise Agent Assist capability that combines dense retrieval, Pinecone vector search, custom re-ranking, and context-aware generation. The system improved answer retrieval accuracy by 12%, reduced average agent call-handling time, and was featured in a public Pinecone customer story.

EKS
Unified Model Deployment Framework

Kubernetes · Model serving · Platform engineering

Developed an EKS-based deployment framework that serves LLMs, ASR, and other ML models behind a single inference gateway. The platform scales to 3M+ requests per day while standardizing orchestration, autoscaling, and per-model deployment workflows.

vLLM
Custom vLLM Serving for 30B Models

LLM infrastructure · PagedAttention · Cost optimization

Wrote custom model architectures for 30B, 8B, and small language models in the vLLM serving framework. Leveraging PagedAttention delivered about 2x throughput improvement over naive KV-cache allocation and enabled cost-effective serving of proprietary models at enterprise scale.

SDK
Enterprise LLM Service Consumption Model

SDKs · Secure auth · Platform adoption

Architected reusable Node.js and Python SDKs with secure token-based authentication for enterprise LLM consumption. The model was adopted by 6+ product teams, eliminated redundant platform builds, and accelerated time-to-production for LLM-powered features.

SUM
Real-Time Advice Call Summarization

Custom LLMs · NLP · Financial advice

Developed a real-time call summarization system for 10K+ daily advice calls, generating summaries within one minute of conversation end using self-hosted custom LLMs and domain-specific business logic. The system achieved 90% summarization accuracy at 30x lower cost compared with commercial solutions.

ASR
Enterprise Speech Analytics Platform

ASR · PII redaction · Speech intelligence

Built and scaled an enterprise speech-to-text and NLP analytics platform with PII redaction, speaker diarization, and sentiment/emotion detection. The platform processes tens of millions of calls per month across multiple lines of business and surfaces actionable insights for operations leaders.

CALM
CALM: Cost-efficient, Model Agnostic, Low-latency Modular LLM Deployment Framework

IEEE AAIML 2026 · Mar 20, 2026 · LLM deployment research

CALM is a purpose-built, cost-aware LLM deployment methodology for production AI teams. It addresses high third-party inference costs, dynamic SLA requirements, and always-on production reliability through dynamic autoscaling, workload-aware dedicated tenancy, and intelligent resource orchestration.

Empirical results demonstrate up to 5x inference cost reduction compared with managed hosted solutions under high-load conditions, while providing practical deployment patterns for scalable, performant, and cost-optimized LLM applications.
