Selected Work & Research

A representative snapshot of enterprise AI systems, applied research, and production architecture work.

ASR
Financial-Domain Automatic Speech Recognition

Speech AI · Privacy-preserving ML · Production platform

Led and contributed to a proprietary ASR capability for sensitive financial conversations. The system achieved 98.7% internal accuracy, outperformed a major cloud transcription baseline, reduced operating cost by roughly 15x, and supported millions of dollars in annual savings while improving privacy controls.

LLM
Enterprise GenAI Platform, Domain LLMs & Protect

Generative AI · PII protection · Responsible AI

Built and scaled internal GenAI capabilities for financial-services workflows, including domain-aligned LLMs, reusable adoption blueprints, and AI-powered data protection for sensitive information. The work helped accelerate safe enterprise adoption while reducing dependency on higher-cost vendor-only patterns.

SUM
Advisor-Client Call Summarization & Agent Assist

NLP · Summarization · Human-in-the-loop AI

Architected AI systems for advisor-client interaction intelligence, including summarization and agent-assist workflows. The work combined engineering design, stakeholder alignment, quality controls, and collaboration with external partners to move from prototype to practical enterprise use.

ROI
DeepCI Cost Intelligence Platform

Analytics · ML systems · Enterprise savings

Contributed to a production cost-intelligence platform that has supported multiple enterprise use cases for years, with reported annualized savings in the $5.5M–$6M range. The platform demonstrates how durable ML systems can compound business value over time.

CALM
CALM-LLM: Cost-Aware LLM Deployment

LLM infrastructure · Autoscaling · Applied research

First-author research on production-centric LLM deployment economics, covering cost-aware scaling, tenancy, cold starts, TTFT/ITL, and SLA-aware orchestration. The work demonstrates up to 5x cost reduction in high-load scenarios and was accepted for an IEEE AAIML oral presentation.

RL
Rubric-Grounded SME Preference Alignment

LLM evaluation · GRPO · SME feedback

Designed a framework for aligning financial-advisory summaries with expert preferences using structured SME feedback, LLM-as-judge rubrics, rubric validation, and GRPO-based fine-tuning. The approach connects product quality signals directly to model optimization.
